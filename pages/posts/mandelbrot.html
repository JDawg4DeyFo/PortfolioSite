Welcome to JDawg's Mandelbrot Dots!

This is my web based implementation of the Mandelbrot Set. It's more of a novelty than anything else, but I still think it's pretty cool.

Controls:

  • Left Click to zoom
  • Ctrl+Click to zoom out
  • Shift+scroll to change zoom increment
  • Press R to reset graph
  • Press . to switch between additive and multiplicative zoom increments.
